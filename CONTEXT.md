# Context

## Terms

### Supplier Submission
A package of supplier-provided input used to start article intake. It can contain label images, PDFs, raw product photos, and manually entered values.

### Article Draft
A structured representation of a product created from the supplier submission and supplier corrections. It is the working draft that can become ready for internal approval.

### Source File
An uploaded file from the supplier submission, such as a label photo, PDF, or raw product photo. Source files are inputs to extraction and validation.

### Display Image
A curated image intended to represent the product in the article draft. Display images are outputs of the intake process, not the full set of uploaded inputs.

### Blocking Issue
A data quality problem that must be resolved before the article draft can become ready for internal approval.

### Ready For Internal Approval
The state where an article draft has passed the agreed intake quality rules and can be handed over to Inkop/Kategori for review.

### Internal Approval
The internal review step performed after the supplier intake flow. It is outside the scope of this POC except for a minimal status view.
